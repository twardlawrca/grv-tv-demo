★ Patient Print Requests ★
How to Request a Print
Send Your Request To
grv-patientprintrequests@recoverycoa.com
1
Open Microsoft Edge and sign in to your personal email.
2
Compose a new email addressed to the print request mailbox above.
3
Use the required subject line format (shown below).
4
Attach or describe what needs to be printed, then send. Staff will be notified.
Required Subject Line Format
First Name · Last Initial · Patient ID# · Case Manager
Example: John D. · 12345 · Devangela Griffin  (Patient ID is on your wristband)
